My Story

I was born in Israel and have been living in the US since 2009. My previous career was in Tech, and though there were many things I loved about it, something was missing for me. In becoming a therapist, I found that in addition to love of logic and thinking, I have a deep interest in relationships and I am committed to unravelling my true self. I am grateful to have my profession be a vessel to my fulfillment of being the human I want to be.
I am a mother of 3, in a long term partnership, I am an immigrant, and identify as a woman.
I am a relationship tender, intuitive healer and a spiritual being. I hold a diverse, open, empathic, non-judgmental, understanding and optimistic view.
I graduated from CIIS in 2016 where I was trained in somatic, transpersonal, humanistic, relational and psychodynamic psychotherapy with an attachment orientation and trauma informed. In addition, I trained in grief counseling at Memorial Hospice in Santa Rosa, California and I am trained in SAT (Somatic Attachment Therapy) which incorporates somatic, attachment and IFS (internal family systems). I am trained in EMDR and believe that this modality is tremendously effective. I have helped people overcome childhood trauma as well as recent traumatic events very quickly and effectively. I offer EMDR sessions but also able to integrate EMDR with the rest of the modalities I work with.
