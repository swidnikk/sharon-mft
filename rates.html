RATES

Individual Therapy

Sessions are 50 minutes via Zoom or in my office in Sebastopol and my fee is 150$ per session. I offer several slots of sliding scale for those in need, feel free to inquire. In addition, if you're an elder, let's talk, I believe in giving back to our elders and would love to work with you.

Couples Counseling

Sessions are 1 hour and 15 minutes and the fee is 200$ a session.

Family sessions

Sessions are 1 and a half to 1 hour 45 minutes and the fee is 240$ a session. Sessions take place via zoom.

Child + Teen Therapy

Sessions are 50 minutes and take place either at the park or at my office. I also offer zoom sessions for teens. The fee is 150$ per session. I offer sliding scale for families in need.
