My Story

My practice is located in Sebastopol, California.
I was born in Israel and have been living in the US since 2009. I have a previous career in Tech, I am a mother, a partner, an immigrant, a woman and a human on this planet. I hold a diverse view, open, and empathic, non-judgmental and understanding.
I graduated from CIIS in 2016 where I was trained in somatic, transpersonal, humanistic, relational and psychodynamic psychotherapy with an attachment orientation and trauma informed. In addition I trained in grief counseling as well as SAT which incorporates somatic, attachment and IFS (internal family systems).
I trained in various schools and offer counseling for teens, a population I deeply appreciate.
In addition, I have deep respect to the elderly population. If you are an elder, please contact me to hear about my specials for you!

If you cannot refuse to fall down,
refuse to stay down.

lift your heart toward heaven,
and like a hungry beggar,
